About Us

CYRE

    CYRE is a cybersecurity company with operations in North America, Europe, and Asia. CYRE was founded by cybersecurity veterans with unparalleled experience in advanced vulnerability research, active cyber defense, multi-tiered intelligence collection and analysis, and offensive operations.

CYRE was built with the goals of aiding governments in protecting their national interests, and supporting organizations in advancing their corporate strategies and proactively defending their assets.

For more information about our corporate services and solutions and our tailored cybersecurity capabilities for governments, please contact us. Access to certain CYRE capabilities and solutions are restricted to organizations with government approval.
