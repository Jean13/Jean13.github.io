Introduction

Named after the great ruler who unified Japan, Tokugawa unifies three elements, Beauty in presentation, Purity in quality and Simplicity in design, to bring together dishes that are culturally important to the island nation and delicious. At Tokugawa, our goal is to delighten your senses in an ambient of elegance and tradition, with dishes worthy of an emperor.
